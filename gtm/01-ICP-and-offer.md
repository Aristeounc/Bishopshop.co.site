# 01 – ICP and Offer (Peitho)

## Primary ICP

- Role: Founders, managers, sales reps, team leads
- Company type: Early-stage startups, remote-first teams, SMBs
- Core pain: Poor communication in high-stakes meetings, lack of real-time feedback, no coaching access

## Secondary ICP

- Role: Individual contributors who want to level up communication
- Core pain: No structured way to practice or improve interpersonal skills

## Peitho Promise

- One-liner: Peitho helps you become a sharper, more confident communicator — one conversation at a time.
- 3 concrete outcomes:
  1. Better clarity and confidence in meetings and presentations.
  2. Real-time AI feedback on your communication patterns.
  3. A repeatable system for improving how you speak, listen, and lead.

## Flagship use cases

1. Founder prepping for a high-stakes pitch or investor meeting.
2. Sales rep rehearsing a discovery call before going live.
3. Manager getting feedback after a difficult 1:1 or team sync.
