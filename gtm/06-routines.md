# 06 – Routines (Peitho)

These are always-on activities to run every week and month, regardless of launch stage.

## Weekly (every week, non-negotiable)

- [ ] Post 2x on LinkedIn or X (1 founder story, 1 product demo or tip)
- [ ] Send 5–10 direct outreach DMs to potential users or collaborators
- [ ] Share 1 short product clip, screenshot, or "build in public" update
- [ ] Review signups, activation rate, and any user feedback
- [ ] Reply to every comment, DM, or email from users

## Bi-weekly

- [ ] Write 1 short community post (Indie Hackers, Reddit, or relevant Slack/Discord)
- [ ] Check 03-channels.csv and submit to 1 new directory or listing
- [ ] Review 07-metrics.md and identify one thing to improve

## Monthly

- [ ] Ship 1 deeper content piece (guide, case study, or "how we built X" post)
- [ ] Reach out to 2–3 newsletters or influencers for a mention or collab
- [ ] Run a mini re-launch or "feature spotlight" on Product Hunt or social
- [ ] Do 2–3 user interviews or feedback calls
- [ ] Update landing page copy based on what's resonating
- [ ] Review and update 01-ICP-and-offer.md if positioning has shifted
