# 05 – 6-Week Alpha/Beta Plan (Peitho)

## Week 1 – Lock ICP, offer, and landing page

- [ ] Finalize ICP and one-liner from 01-ICP-and-offer.md
- [ ] Ship basic landing page with waitlist/signup
- [ ] Write outreach DM template for alpha invites
- [ ] Identify 20 alpha candidates (personal network)

## Week 2 – Run alpha outreach

- [ ] Send 20 personal DMs to alpha candidates
- [ ] Onboard first 10 users manually
- [ ] Set up feedback form (Typeform or Notion)
- [ ] Hold 3–5 short feedback calls (15 min each)

## Week 3 – Iterate from alpha feedback

- [ ] Fix top 3 friction points from feedback
- [ ] Refine onboarding based on where users got confused
- [ ] Capture 1–2 strong quotes or stories for social proof
- [ ] Prep beta positioning and community post copy

## Week 4 – Open beta

- [ ] Post on Indie Hackers (build-in-public format)
- [ ] Submit to Peerlist, Betalist, Uneed, Microlaunch
- [ ] Post founding story on LinkedIn and X
- [ ] Onboard first 50 beta users

## Week 5 – Beta activation and retention check

- [ ] Check: are 40%+ of beta users returning within 7 days?
- [ ] Send Day 3 and Day 7 email nudges
- [ ] Start collecting testimonials from active users
- [ ] Submit to 3–5 more directories from 03-channels.csv

## Week 6 – Prep v1 big push

- [ ] Finalize Product Hunt assets (tagline, copy, screenshots, GIF)
- [ ] Schedule Product Hunt launch date
- [ ] Write pre-launch LinkedIn/X posts
- [ ] Reach out to 3–5 newsletters or communities for launch support
- [ ] Brief 5–10 supporters to upvote and comment on launch day
