# 02 – Launch Ladders (Peitho)

## Alpha (10–20 users)

- Goal: Deep feedback on core flows, validate ICP pain points
- Who: Founders, devs, managers in your personal network
- Channels: Direct DMs (LinkedIn, X, iMessage), Discord, Slack communities
- Success: 10+ users complete a Peitho session and give written feedback

## Beta (50–100 users)

- Goal: Validate retention and activation, refine onboarding
- Who: Indie Hackers, ProductHunt Ship waitlist, AI tool early adopters
- Channels: Indie Hackers, Peerlist, Betalist, Uneed, AI/productivity communities
- Success: 40%+ of beta users return for a second session within 7 days

## v1 Big Push (250–1000 users)

- Goal: Repeatable acquisition, word-of-mouth, press/directory placement
- Who: Broader ICP (founders, sales, managers, remote teams)
- Channels: Product Hunt launch, AI directories (TAAFT, Futurepedia), LinkedIn content, founder newsletters
- Success: 500+ signups, 30%+ activation rate, 10%+ week-4 retention
