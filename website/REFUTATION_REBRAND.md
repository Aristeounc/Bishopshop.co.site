# Refutation Brand Redesign - Complete Rebrand

## Overview
The website has been completely rebranded from **Abacus** (conversation practice) to **Refutation** (debate mastery platform). This comprehensive rebrand includes updated colors, messaging, scenarios, pricing, and positioning across all pages.

---

## 🎨 Brand Identity

### Color Palette Update
**New Refutation Colors:**
- **Primary**: `#DC2626` (Victory Red) - Bold, confident debate mastery
- **Accent**: `#F59E0B` (Gold Strike) - Precision in winning arguments  
- **Energy**: `#7C3AED` (Deep Purple) - Intellect and debate expertise
- **Surface**: `#0F172A` (Midnight Navy) - Professional debate environment
- **Light**: `#F8FAFC` (Off-white) - Clarity in arguments
- **Neutral**: `#64748B` (Slate) - Balanced perspective

**Why This Palette?**
- Red represents victory and confidence in debate
- Gold represents precision and winning  
- Purple represents intellect and mastery
- Creates sharp, high-contrast, professional aesthetic

### Debate Scenarios (Updated from Conversations)
1. **Policy Debate** - Defend/challenge policy positions with logical arguments
2. **Spotting Fallacies** - Identify and refute logical fallacies
3. **Public Debate** - Formal debate with cross-examination and rebuttals
4. **Business Pitch Defense** - Defend ideas against investor objections
5. **Tournament Debate** - High-pressure competitive scenarios
6. **Philosophical Debate** - Abstract argumentation on ethics and truth

### Value Propositions
1. **AI Debate Opponent** - Claude challenges arguments and identifies fallacies
2. **Competitive Debate** - Challenge opponents with ELO ratings and tournaments
3. **Argumentation Mastery** - Track argument quality, fallacy detection, persuasion

### Pricing Tiers
- **Novice** ($0) - Free tier with 3 debates/month
- **Champion** ($11.99) - Unlimited debates, tournaments, ELO ratings, strategy coaching

### How It Works
1. **Select a Debate** - Policy, fallacies, public debate, business pitch, tournaments
2. **Debate the AI** - Face Claude who challenges weak reasoning
3. **Get Analyzed** - Detailed feedback on argument strength and fallacy detection
4. **Climb Rankings** - Earn ELO ratings and compete globally
