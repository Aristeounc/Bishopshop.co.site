**Real Sparring.** Six AI personas—The Closer, The Guilt-Tripper, The Stonewaller, The Authority, The Charmer, The Negotiator—each designed to put real conversational pressure on you. Not to trick you. To train you.

We've made a public commitment: there's a **Will-Not-Train** list.

Influence without coercion. That's the entire framework.
