FAQ

Common
questions

What domains can I train in?

Salary negotiation, sales, leadership, conflict resolution, dating and relationships, parenting, manipulation defense, and more. New scenarios are added regularly based on our training curriculum roadmap.

How does real-time coach feedback work?

After each sparring exchange, the AI coach evaluates your response against the current skill framework — scoring faithfulness, technique usage, and emotional regulation. You get specific, actionable notes, not generic praise.

What is the ELO system?

Like chess ratings, your ELO score in each of 8 skill tracks changes based on session performance. It's a honest signal of where you actually stand — not where you hope you are.

Is the app available on Android?

Currently, Attune AI is available exclusively on the iOS App Store. Android development is in the roadmap — subscribe to updates to know when it launches.

What is Spot-the-Influence mode?

A unique Attune feature where you watch a conversation scenario unfold and identify influence and manipulation tactics in real time. Builds awareness as a defense skill, not an attack skill.

Can I cancel or pause my subscription?

Yes, anytime. If you need a break, our humane streak model includes freeze slots so you don't lose progress from life getting in the way. No punishment for being human.
