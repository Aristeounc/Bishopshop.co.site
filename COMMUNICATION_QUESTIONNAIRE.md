# Attune AI: Communication Skills Questionnaire

Take this questionnaire before you start training on Attune, and again after 30 days. This will help you track real growth across the skill dimensions that matter.

**Time to complete:** 5–7 minutes  
**Your answers are private.** They're only for you to understand where you're starting.

## Scoring & Interpretation

**Count your C responses.** This is your baseline.

- **0–3 C responses:** New to formal communication training; lots of room to grow.
- **4–7 C responses:** Some solid skills; specific areas to develop.
- **8–12 C responses:** Strong foundation; ready to refine and specialize.
- **13–18 C responses:** Advanced; use Attune to maintain and deepen.
