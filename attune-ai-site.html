Articulation Training

Say what you mean.
Mean what you say.

The Core Gym trains you to read conversations and respond under pressure. Articulation Training goes deeper — rebuilding how you structure thoughts, hold your line, and land your point with the precision of a courtroom advocate, the clarity of a media-trained executive, and the composure of a seasoned negotiator.

🌿

Gentle Mode

Low Pressure

Guided prompts with supportive, encouraging feedback. Build confidence with new frameworks or warm up before a high-stakes moment. The coach scaffolds your thinking and suggests improvements without judgment.

  • Scaffolded sentence starters
  • Tone and pacing suggestions
  • Encouraging corrections
  • Supportive audience simulation
🎯

Standard Mode

Moderate Pressure

Real-time coaching with direct, specific feedback. The coach flags filler words, detects hedging, analyzes your sentence structure, and scores your clarity live. No hand-holding — honest assessment.

  • Live clarity scoring
  • Filler and hedge detection
  • Structure and flow analysis
  • Neutral audience simulation
🔥

Intensive Mode

High Pressure

Adversarial, real-world conditions. The coach interrupts mid-sentence, challenges weak phrasing, imposes time pressure, and demands precision. You lose points for vague language. No mercy.

  • Active interruption challenges
  • Point-loss on vague language
  • Rapid-fire rebuttals
  • Hostile audience simulation

Parent & Child Training

Hear them out.
Help them grow.

The hardest conversations happen at home. Parent & Child Training uses attachment science, Gottman emotion coaching, and developmental neuroscience to rebuild how you connect — from toddler meltdowns to teenage door slams. Practice the moments that matter most before they happen.

💚

Emotion Coach

Gottman 5-Step

Learn to notice your child's emotions before they escalate. The coach walks you through Gottman's five steps: awareness, connection, listening, naming feelings, and problem-solving together. Build emotional literacy for both of you.

  • Emotion awareness prompts
  • Feeling-word vocabulary builder
  • Co-regulation practice
  • Problem-solving scaffolds
🧠

Age Adapter

Developmental Science

The same words don't work at every age. Age Adapter adjusts your language, expectations, and approach based on your child's developmental stage — from concrete toddler thinking to abstract teen reasoning.

  • Stage-matched vocabulary
  • Developmental expectation checks
  • Brain-state awareness cues
  • Autonomy calibration
🩹

Repair Lab

Rupture-Repair Science

Every parent loses it sometimes. Repair Lab teaches you how to come back after you've yelled, dismissed, or disconnected. Based on Tronick's still-face research and Siegel's interpersonal neurobiology — repair is the skill that matters most.

  • Rupture recognition signals
  • Repair script generator
  • Reconnection timing coach
  • Trust-rebuilding patterns

FAQ

Common
questions

What domains can I train in?

Salary negotiation, sales, leadership, conflict resolution, dating and relationships, parenting, manipulation defense, and more. New scenarios are added regularly based on the expert curriculum roadmap.

How does real-time coach feedback work?

After each sparring exchange, the AI coach evaluates your response against the current skill framework — scoring faithfulness, technique usage, and emotional regulation. You get specific, actionable notes, not generic praise.

What is the ELO system?

Like chess ratings, your ELO score in each of 8 skill tracks changes based on session performance. It's a honest signal of where you actually stand — not where you hope you are.

Is the app available on Android?

Currently, Attune AI is available exclusively on the iOS App Store. Android development is in the roadmap — subscribe to updates to know when it launches.

What is Spot-the-Influence mode?

A unique Attune feature where you watch a conversation scenario unfold and identify influence and manipulation tactics in real time. Builds awareness as a defense skill, not an attack skill.

Can I cancel or pause my subscription?

Yes, anytime. If you need a break, our humane streak model includes freeze slots so you don't lose progress from life getting in the way. No punishment for being human.
